Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is a Student Visa?

    Student visa is issued to a person who are planning to study in a foreign country. This visa only allows limited hours for work. you have to have a place of study for the approval of student visa.

  • Should I pay for the school to obtain student visa?

    Yes, you have to pay for 1 year of study or 2 years of study depending on the school you choose in order to be qualified for an student visa.

  • What is the difference between student and working visa?

    Working visa allows you to be employed full time while student visa only allows you to work limited amount of hours a week.
